about

cardnected

cardnected is a "conversation card" game platform providing sets of deep questions designed to build up a valuable experience by creating a meaningful conversation.

We aim to remove the barriers to building new or strengthening the existing social connections online, Enabling Happiness and Productivity at Home #StayAtHome.

the "rules"

how to play

  • Ask anyone to join you to play the game together.
  • Set up any video conference call
  • Customize your game here, then
  • Share your screen so the other player can pick the cards
    • Enjoy the experience!

While the rules of the game are really up to you, we do have a few suggestions for how to play.

Classic

Each take turns choosing a card, then everyone answers the same question

Popcorn

Have everyone pick a different question each, and then take turns answering them.
